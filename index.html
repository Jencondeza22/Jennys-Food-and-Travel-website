Grahe Sidewalk Grill Restaurant

Grahe Gingoog

Grahe Sidewalk Grill is a hidden culinary gem that beckons to all barbecue enthusiasts and food lovers alike. Nestled in a charming sidewalk setting, this grill restaurant offers a delightful escape where the aroma of sizzling meats and the sounds of crackling flames create an inviting ambiance for diners. At Grahe Sidewalk Grill, the menu is a tantalizing showcase of smoky delights and savory treats. From perfectly charred barbecue ribs and juicy grilled chicken to flavorful burgers and delectable sides, every dish is a celebration of the art of grilling. Whether you're craving a hearty barbecue feast or a casual meal with friends, Grahe Sidewalk Grill promises a memorable dining experience that will leave your taste buds tingling with satisfaction. Step into Grahe Sidewalk Grill and embark on a culinary journey where the flavors of the grill take center stage. Indulge in the smoky goodness of expertly grilled meats and the comforting warmth of a welcoming atmosphere, all while enjoying the simple pleasures of good food and great company. It's not just a meal; it's a barbecue experience to savor and enjoy..

Food they offer

The barbecue at Grahe Sidewalk Grill is a true delight for the senses. Each bite of their perfectly grilled meats is a symphony of smoky flavors, tender textures, and mouthwatering juiciness. Whether you're indulging in their succulent barbecue ribs, flavorful grilled chicken, or charred skewers of marinated goodness, the barbecue at Grahe Sidewalk Grill is sure to leave you craving for more. Their sisig is a culinary masterpiece that combines crispy and sizzling pork bits with tangy and savory flavors. The dish is a harmonious blend of textures and tastes, with each bite offering a delightful crunch and a burst of umami goodness. Served hot and fresh, Grahe Sidewalk Grill's sisig is a must-try for those seeking a unique and delicious Filipino dish. For those craving a taste of the orient, the tanghon and pancit at Grahe Sidewalk Grill are sure to satisfy. The tanghon, with its delicate glass noodles and flavorful stir-fried vegetables, is a light and refreshing option that is perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. On the other hand, the pancit is a hearty and comforting dish that showcases the rich flavors of Filipino cuisine, with its savory noodles, tender meats, and fresh vegetables. Overall, the food at Grahe Sidewalk Grill is a culinary experience not to be missed. From their mouthwatering barbecue to their delectable sisig, tanghon, and pancit, each dish is a testament to the restaurant's commitment to quality, flavor, and customer satisfaction. Indulge in a feast of flavors and textures at Grahe Sidewalk Grill and discover why it is a favorite destination for barbecue and Filipino cuisine enthusiasts alike.

Paolo's Beach Resort

Me Myself and I

Paolo's Beach Resort is a hidden gem nestled in the heart of our barangay, offering a slice of paradise that feels like a mini Boracay right at our doorstep. As you step onto the pristine shores of the resort, you are greeted by the soft, powdery sand and the gentle lull of the waves, transporting you to a tranquil oasis of relaxation and beauty. Just like Boracay, Paolo's Beach Resort boasts crystal-clear waters that shimmer under the sun, inviting you to take a refreshing dip or simply bask in the warmth of the tropical sun. The palm-fringed coastline provides the perfect backdrop for a leisurely stroll or a peaceful moment of solitude, allowing you to reconnect with nature and unwind in a serene setting. Whether you're looking to swim, sunbathe, or simply enjoy a beachside picnic with loved ones, Paolo's Beach Resort offers a little slice of paradise where you can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life. With its Boracay-like charm and local flair, this resort is a true haven for those seeking a tranquil retreat and a taste of tropical paradise right in our own barangay
